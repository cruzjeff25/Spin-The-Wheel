How to Modify the Probabilities:

Locate the prizes array in the script tag. 

You can alter the win chance by changing the value of the weight property.

The logic handles calculations automatically, meaning the total weights do not need to add up to 100.

If you set Grand Prize to 1 and Better Luck to 99, the algorithm calculates a 1% chance for the Grand Prize and 99% for Better Luck.

The wheel draws equal slices visually to keep players unaware of the rigged mathematical probabilities.
